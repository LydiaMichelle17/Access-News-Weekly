# AGENTS.md

## Project

This repository contains Access News Weekly, a special education current-events curriculum platform.

The first demo is a manual-first, API-free public website with a sample Friday Five issue. The future production version should become a Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Supabase, Vercel-ready publishing platform.

## Product Principles

- Build for accessibility first.
- Keep the tone warm, teacher-created, and professional.
- Do not use babyish design.
- Do not make AI the central brand message.
- Use the phrase "IEP-aligned progress monitoring support," not "IEP compliance."
- Do not collect student personal information.
- Keep the app manual-first and AI-ready.

## Accessibility Expectations

- Semantic HTML.
- Keyboard-accessible components.
- Clear focus states.
- Proper heading hierarchy.
- High contrast.
- No rapid animations.
- No autoplay audio or video.
- Print-friendly issue pages.

## Safety and Privacy

- Do not create student accounts yet.
- Do not collect student names.
- Do not collect IEP documents.
- Do not store student quiz results yet.
- Do not add third-party ads.

## AI Integration

Do not connect the OpenAI API until explicitly instructed. Create placeholders only.
